About

I've always enjoyed facing challenges and finding solutions to problems ahead. I also appreciate learning and constant development. Despite my humanities background, I never shied away from logical and analytical thinking. My search for an area that would allow me to consolidate those ambitions has led me to programming: field dynamic enough to provide me with years, if not decades, of opportunities. For now, I am looking for employment as a junior frontend developer, in order to hone my skills and gain an insight in a professional environment. In the long run, I would like to pick one technology - either a language or a framework - and focus on it, getting to know every nook and cranny.
